Greek Lexicon of the Roman and Byzantine Periods from B. C. 146 to A. D. 1100

Sophocles Evangelinos Apostolides

Sophocles Evangelinos Apostolides (1807-1888) was an eminent Greek scholar of the 19th century, a lexicographer and one of the first Greek professors at Harvard University. Sophocles Evangelinos Apostolidis, apart from his many didactic writings, wrote and published in 1870 the valuable dictionary of the Greek language of the Roman and Byzantine periods, which covers the time period from 146 BC. until 1100.

The versions of the dictionary are:
E. A. Sophocles: Greek Lexicon of the Roman and Byzantine Periods from B. C. 146 to A. D. 1100, New York: Charles Scribner's Sons 1887. Nachdruck: Cambridge: Harvard University Press 1914; Hildesheim: Olms 1975, 1992; Kessinger Publishing 2004, Part One: ISBN 1-417-94793-4; Part Two: ISBN 1-417-94794-2
